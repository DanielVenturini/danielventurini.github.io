Irregular verbs

The study of irregular verbs is one of the most important parts on study of English. At begginer, the irregular verbs are very akward, but they can be understood with strive. However, when they are studied in pieces, that is, studying the similar ones, it can help a lot. The following study is focused in study the irregular verbs by following their pattern. There are about 135 verbs divided into eight class of pattern. Actually, the last class contains the verbs that don't have any pattern.
  • verbs ending with ght (7 verbs).
  • verbs that are written in the same way in infinitive and past tense (17 verbs).
  • verbs that a double e or o were replaced by a single e or o, respectively (6 verbs).
  • verbs with ep replaced by a et (4 verbs).
  • verbs with y replaced by a id (3 verbs).
  • verbs with ear replaced by a ore (4 verbs).
  • verbs with a single word changed (59 verbs).
  • verbs that don't have any pattern (35 verbs).
Finally, there is a challenge:
  • all of the 135 verbs above in the alphabetic order.
